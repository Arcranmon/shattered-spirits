Despite the unpredictability of the Shattered World, the seasons are remarkably consistent. As the year passes the dominant spiritual energy changes, following a consistent cycle of Wood, Earth, Fire, Metal, Air, Water. The year is divided into 12 months, alternating between 31 day months where two elements are co-dominant and 29 day months where a singular element reigns supreme. While dominant, forces related to that element are enhanced and unpredictable; while fire is dominant, for example, flammable objects might spontaneously combust and flames might move like they are a truly living force.
