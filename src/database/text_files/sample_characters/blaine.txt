### Blaine and Fennek
*A bookish, quiet boy who carefully studies the intricacies of spirits and has one of the most dangerous, violent flame spirits within Serpent Village.*
&nbsp;
The current frontrunner for the next Head Keeper in Serpent Village, Blaine is the most studious of his generation. He has read every book in the village ten times over, and prides himself on his comprehensive knowledge of the heroes of Serpent Village, a crucial skill in his keeper training. He is close friends with Margaret, but tends to butt heads with Erika.
&nbsp;
As one of the older villages within the Greenvale, the keepers of Serpent Village have a much more difficult task than others. They must memorize and recount as much of the history of those that came before as possible in order to ensure that their spirits remain within the town and continue to provide their assistance. Blaine has fully dedicated himself to this task. He studies constantly, and has earned a reputation as an adept storyteller.
&nbsp;
To most, Blaine's goal is simply to ensure that he is the head keeper, although he is always looking for new ways to ensure spirits stay within the village, although he has a secret, secondary goal: to learn how to ensure a spirit does not linger. Fennek, Blaine's spirit, is a creature of rare violence and cruelty and, while the two share the same bond as other spiritbound, Blaine is terrified of what havoc Fennek would wreak without him there to keep him in line. While he would never admit it, Blaine resents the spirit he was granted, and hates the wild mane of bright red hair and violently long incisors the bond has granted him.
&nbsp;
Fennek takes the form a flaming cat with a long, scorpion-like tail and wicked claws. He seems to have absorbed the worst qualities of both flames and cats, and enjoys hunting and destruction seemingly for the thrill of it. Children within the town have learned to give Fennek a wide berth. Fennek loves (hunting) insects and wood spirits and hates any form of perfume.
