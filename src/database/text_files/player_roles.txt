In order to help the game run more smoothly, some responsibilities that might be the GMs in a different TTRPG are relegated to the players to help the game flow better. These roles are independent of what the characters are capable of and, while they are not necessary, players should do their best to assist with keeping the game flowing. Not every role needs to be filled, especially for small parties, but players should feel free to adopt multiple roles.

&nbsp;

### Cartographer
The Cartographer is the one in charge of tracking the party's progress on individual Legs on a Journey, as well as tracking any interesting locations the party has found on that Leg. As the party gains knowledge of routes and finds more locations the Cartographer's log will grow, charting the party's increase in skill.

&nbsp;

### Matchmaker
Characters in Shattered Spirits have Bonds, which can be spent for advantages. Generally, players track their own Bonds but, for ease of use, the Matchmaker tracks the Bonds within the party, as well as any Bonds that affect the whole party.

&nbsp;

### Quartermaster
The Camp Phase can have significant inventory management and the Quartermaster's job is to make sure that everything goes smoothly. They help make sure that food is marked off, characters gain or lose Stress from the quality of their food, and otherwise ensure a smooth Camp Phase.
